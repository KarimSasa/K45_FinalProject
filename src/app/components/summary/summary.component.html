AI Market Growth Projections

Market Analysis 2024-2034

The market analysis reveals substantial growth projections for both Edge AI and Generative AI sectors. The Edge AI market, currently valued at USD 21 billion, is expected to experience significant expansion, reaching USD 140 billion by 2034. This represents a remarkable growth trajectory over the decade, highlighting the increasing adoption of edge computing solutions across industries.

Similarly, the Generative AI market shows even more dramatic growth potential, with projections indicating an increase from its current value of USD 36 billion to an impressive USD 356 billion by 2030. This nearly tenfold increase reflects the rapidly growing demand for generative AI capabilities across various sectors and applications.

Source: Wevolver Technology Report 2024
